A program of the nation, for the nation and by the nation for managing continuity of education

Teachers, public & social organizations, individuals and other experts across states and UTs have been developing educational content, but in silos, with minimal opportunity to share with the larger community or receive the required recognition.

VidyaDaan is a program to bring together these well-meaning experts and resourceful individuals and organizations on a common platform to develop and contribute content in alignment with the needs of school education and be recognized nationally. It is therefore a call to all Indians to engage in developing our future citizens by contributing educational resources.

VidyaDaan creates a structured warehouse of crowd sourced content that can be leveraged by Centre and State for school education. Each contribution shall be reviewed and available to any board/state/UT to pick up and use in their own vertical on DIKSHA.
